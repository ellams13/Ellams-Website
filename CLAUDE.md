# **Ellams — Claude / AI Development Guide**

# Personal Design & Working Preferences

## Design Identity

Ellams is a modern editorial publication focused on architecture, objects, and environments.

It should feel:

- restrained
- confident
- minimal
- structured

Not:

- loud
- trendy
- overly branded
- like a startup landing page

---

## Visual Taste

### Composition

- Prefer centered or symmetrical compositions
- Clean lines and strong geometry
- Avoid chaotic or overly busy visuals

### Image Selection

- Consistent tone and lighting
- Neutral / warm palettes
- Architectural and material-focused

---

## Copy Style

- Short, declarative sentences
- No marketing language
- No “taste / noise” phrasing
- Minimal words

Examples:

- "Built Form"
- "Interiors"
- "Objects"

---

## Layout Preferences

- Grid-based systems
- Consistent spacing
- Occasional variation (larger central image)
- Strong vertical rhythm

---

## Development Philosophy

- Build simple first, refine later
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Prioritize design over features
- Remove anything that feels generic or template-like

## **1\. Purpose**

This file guides AI-assisted development of the Ellams website.

The goal is to build a clean, editorial-style website with strong design consistency and minimal complexity.

---

## **2\. Core Principles**

### **2.1 Design First**

* Prioritize visual hierarchy over features
* Keep UI minimal
* Use large typography and spacing

---

### **2.2 No Feature Bloat**

* Do not add:
  * likes
  * comments
  * profiles
* This is NOT a social app

---

### **2.3 Consistency Over Creativity**

* Reuse components
* Maintain consistent spacing, colors, and typography

---

## **3\. Tech Stack (Recommended)**

* Frontend: Next.js
* Styling: Tailwind CSS
* Content: Static JSON or simple CMS (Notion or local data)

---

## **4\. Component Structure**

### **Core Components**

* Navbar
* SidebarMenu (slide-out)
* HeroSection
* ContentGrid
* ContentCard
* PostPage
* Footer

---

## **5\. Styling Rules**

### **Colors**

* Primary: \#2B67A3
* Background: \#F5F5F0
* Text: \#111111

---

### **Typography**

* Use bold sans-serif fonts
* Headings should be large and dominant
* Avoid decorative fonts

---

### **Layout**

* Use grid systems
* Maintain generous padding (at least 24–48px)
* Avoid clutter

---

## **6\. Content Pipeline (AI-Assisted)**

### **Input**

Raw content (links, images, videos)

### **AI Tasks**

* Generate short editorial captions
* Tag categories (architecture, fashion, objects)
* Maintain consistent tone

---

### **Caption Style**

* Short (1–2 sentences)
* Descriptive, not casual
* No emojis or slang

Example:
"Concrete minimalism defines this urban stadium, balancing mass with precision."

---

## **7\. Development Phases**

### **Phase 1 (MVP)**

* Static homepage
* Content grid
* Post pages
* Basic navigation

---

### **Phase 2**

* Sidebar animation
* Category filtering
* Improved typography

---

### **Phase 3**

* CMS integration
* TikTok content integration

---

## **8\. What to Avoid**

* Overengineering backend
* Adding unnecessary animations
* Using too many colors
* Making it feel like a social app

---

## **9\. Definition of Done**

* Site loads fast
* Design matches reference quality
* Content feels curated
* UI is clean and minimal
