# **Typography System**

## **Style Direction**

* Bold, editorial, architectural  
* Sans-serif  
* Tight spacing

## **Headings**

* Large (48px–96px)  
* Bold weight (700+)

## **Body Text**

* Minimal usage  
* Clean and readable

## **Inspiration**

* End-To-End Tech (bold minimal)  
* Maben (strong identity)
