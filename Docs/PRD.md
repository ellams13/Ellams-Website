

# **Ellams — Product Requirements Document (PRD)**

## **1\. Overview**

**Ellams** is a curated digital magazine for modern male taste.  
It focuses on architecture, fashion, objects (watches, cars), and cultural spaces.

The product is not social. It is editorial.

**Core idea:**

Taste, in a world of noise.

---

## **2\. Goals**

### **Primary Goal**

Build a visually compelling, high-performance website that showcases curated content in a clean, editorial format.

### **Secondary Goals**

* Learn design systems and “vibe coding”  
* Understand content curation pipelines  
* Create a brand foundation for future expansion (TikTok \+ store)

---

## **3\. Target User**

**Profile:**

* Male, 18–35  
* Interested in fashion, architecture, watches, culture  
* Wants to feel “ahead” and refined  
* Consumes TikTok/Instagram but craves higher-quality content

**Mindset:**

* Values aesthetics over volume  
* Wants inspiration, not noise  
* Doesn’t want to dig for good content

---

## **4\. Value Proposition**

A curated, editorial experience delivering high-quality cultural content with strong visual taste and minimal noise.

---

## **5\. Core Features (MVP)**

### **5.1 Homepage**

* Large hero text (bold typography)  
* Minimal intro  
* Grid of curated content

---

### **5.2 Content Grid**

* Clean layout (2–4 columns)  
* Large imagery  
* No clutter (no likes, no comments)

---

### **5.3 Post Page**

* Full-width image/video  
* Short editorial caption  
* Category label

---

### **5.4 Sidebar Menu**

* Slide-out from left (30–40% width)  
* Links:  
  * Home  
  * Architecture  
  * Fashion  
  * Objects  
  * About

---

### **5.5 Footer Section**

* Full-width blue block (\#2B67A3)  
* Large statement text  
* Minimal navigation \+ email signup

---

## **6\. Design System**

### **Colors**

* Primary: \#2B67A3 (blue)  
* Background: off-white (\#F5F5F0)  
* Text: near-black (\#111111)

---

### **Typography**

* Bold, large headings  
* Sans-serif  
* Tight spacing (editorial feel)

---

### **Layout Principles**

* High whitespace  
* Strong hierarchy  
* Minimal UI elements  
* Grid-based structure

---

## **7\. Content Strategy**

### **Source**

* Curated from:  
  * Instagram  
  * YouTube  
  * Pinterest  
  * Brand campaigns

### **Process**

1. Discover content  
2. Filter by taste  
3. Add editorial caption (AI-assisted)  
4. Categorize  
5. Publish

---

### **Taste Rules**

Content must:

* Be visually high quality  
* Avoid mainstream/hype content  
* Fit minimalist / architectural aesthetic

---

## **8\. Out of Scope (MVP)**

* User accounts  
* Comments or likes  
* Upload system  
* Complex CMS  
* Full ecommerce store

---

## **9\. Future Expansion**

### **TikTok**

* Short-form curated clips  
* Drive traffic to site

### **Store**

* Limited drops  
* Taste-aligned products (posters, apparel, objects)

---

## **10\. Success Criteria**

* Clean, cohesive design  
* Fast load time  
* Strong visual identity  
* Content feels curated and intentional
